# Home Front Solutions 30-Day Growth Plan

## Goal

Drive more qualified clicks and more job applications from:

- Google Jobs
- local SEO
- young-professional search intent
- free community distribution

## Core targets

- increase impressions on job pages
- increase clicks to city pages and candidate-intent articles
- increase completed applications
- increase branded searches for Home Front Solutions

## Week 1

- Deploy the latest SEO and job-feed branch.
- Set up or verify Google Search Console.
- Submit:
  - `https://www.homefrontsolutionsllc.com/sitemap.xml`
  - `https://www.homefrontsolutionsllc.com/job-sitemap.xml`
- Request indexing for:
  - homepage
  - careers page
  - all active job pages
  - Greensboro, High Point, Winston-Salem, Raleigh, and Charlotte market pages
  - newest young-professional articles
- Fully update Google Business Profile:
  - categories
  - description
  - phone
  - hours
  - website
  - services
  - photos

## Week 2

- Publish LinkedIn and Instagram posts using the outreach pack.
- Share job links in free local/community channels.
- Ask every strong rep, leader, and friend-of-brand to repost one role.
- Start collecting reviews from:
  - applicants with positive interview experience
  - partners
  - customers where appropriate

## Week 3

- Review Search Console data.
- Identify the pages getting impressions but low CTR.
- Improve titles and descriptions where needed.
- Publish one more city or young-professional article based on real search/query data.
- Post one short Google Business Profile update focused on hiring or local opportunity.

## Week 4

- Double down on the market pages with the best impressions.
- Push the best-performing job to all free social/community channels again with a different angle.
- Add internal links from high-impression articles to the best converting job pages.
- Review applications, clicks, and CTR and decide which city or topic deserves the next content page.

## Exact content topics to prioritize

- best jobs for young professionals in Greensboro NC
- recent grad sales jobs in Raleigh NC
- communication skills jobs for college grads in Charlotte
- how young professionals build income fast in field sales
- best jobs for ambitious 18 to 25 year olds in North Carolina
- jobs that build confidence and communication skills after college

## Distribution targets

- LinkedIn company page
- founder LinkedIn profile
- employee reposts
- Instagram feed and stories
- Facebook page and local groups
- local college career groups
- alumni groups
- city community groups
- referral text/DM outreach

## Success criteria after 30 days

- more indexed pages
- higher impressions on market pages and job pages
- better CTR from search
- more direct apply clicks
- more completed applications
